What We Do

Sell-side advisory. Select buy-side mandates.

Sell-side advisory is our primary focus. We also represent a small number of buyers with active acquisition mandates.

Positioning the business
We assess the business honestly and present it compellingly, financials, narrative, and deal structure, before a single buyer is approached.
Managing the process
We control the flow of information, manage buyer interactions, and keep the process moving. Vendors focus on running their business while we run the deal.
Negotiating outcomes
Price matters. So does structure, timing, and what happens at and after settlement. We negotiate all of it.

Qualified buyers only

About Blackmont

Built out of frustration with how deals were being handled.

The $10M–$30M segment has always been an awkward fit. Too large for generalist brokers. Too small for large advisory firms to give it real attention.

The result is a junior advisor, a listing, and a process that drifts. Blackmont was built as the alternative.

—
Our philosophy
Know your buyers before you need them. Run a disciplined process. Tell clients what they need to hear. We'd rather decline a mandate than run one that won't close.
—
Discretion as a default
Every engagement is confidential from the first conversation. We don't discuss clients or publicise mandates.
—
A small firm by choice
We could take on more clients. The quality of our work depends on not doing so.
